## Runtime speech

- Snow Peak dialogue uses the browser or operating system's generic Chinese
  speech-synthesis voice at runtime.
- No celebrity recording, scraped clip, cloned voice model, or redistributable
  speech asset is bundled with the game.
- The line `张雪峰老师，我还记得你` is delivered as a pitch-stepped generic
  Chinese speech-synthesis sequence over an original four-note oscillator cue.
  It does not use the recording, melody, accompaniment, or a cloned performer
  voice from `《念张师》`.
